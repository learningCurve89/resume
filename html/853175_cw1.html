Biography

Born in a Land far far away in 1989, Fran is an outgoing and very organised little fellow. The legend says she can be found roaming the corridors at night thinking about time management and how many A4 papers can fit in one file before it becomes messy.

In the year 2005 she began studying languages under the supervision of Nuns. They were quite keen on passing down the love for discipline, which she, on the other hand, did not enjoy as much. But the teachings helped with her organization skills, time management, avoidance of procrastination and many other supepowers.

In the following years she embarked in her first academic adventure, studying humanities in Rome. During this time she left for Danmark to never return! And after 6 months she returned and completed her studies.

Next she embarked on a journey to the United Kingdom, and after landing in London, she ran for the quiet hills of Aberystwyht North Wales. It's here that she completed her MA in Politics Media and Performance, to finally understand that the humanities are a saturated field and move on to the next adventure.

And when Aberystywyth became to small to handle her, she moved on to Cardiff where she conquered the corporate world and got herself the best job ever at GoCompare where she could finally learn new skills and get ready to become the Queen of Coding!
